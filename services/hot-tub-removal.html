Why Professional Hot Tub Removal Makes Sense

  • Heavy & Difficult: Hot tubs weigh 500–1,500 lbs. Moving them requires special equipment and multiple people.
  • Electrical Hazards: Electrical connections must be safely disconnected. Improper handling risks injury or damage.
  • Water Drainage: Contains 200–1,000+ gallons of water that must be properly drained and disposed of.
  • Tight Spaces: Hot tubs rarely fit through gates or doorways intact. Disassembly is often required.
  • Yard Damage: DIY removal often damages landscaping, decking, or gates. Professionals do it right the first time.

Professional removal costs $300–$600. Attempting it yourself risks injury, property damage, and hours of frustration. Call Clear the Clutter instead.
