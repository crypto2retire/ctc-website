Standard Junk Removal Pricing in Oshkosh & Appleton

Clear the Clutter uses volume-based pricing, which means you pay based on how much space your junk takes up in our truck. Here's what you can expect:

  • Small Load (under 1/8 truck): $150–$200. Good for a few items like a mattress, couch, or armchair.
  • Medium Load (1/8 to 1/4 truck): $300–$450. Typical garage or basement cleanout with multiple pieces of furniture and miscellaneous items.

These are typical ranges for Oshkosh, Appleton, and the surrounding Fox Valley area. Your exact quote depends on several factors.

What Factors Affect Your Junk Removal Cost?

1. Volume and Weight

The primary cost driver is how much space your items occupy in our truck. A single leather sofa costs differently than three filing cabinets, even if they take up similar space. Heavy items like cast iron stoves, old safes, or full appliances are priced accordingly.

2. Accessibility

If your junk is on the first floor near the curb, loading is fast and inexpensive. If items are in a basement, attic, or behind obstacles, it takes more time and crew effort. Stairs, narrow doorways, and cluttered paths can increase the cost by 10–30%.

3. Hazardous Materials

Some items require special handling. Electronics, refrigerators with Freon, batteries, paint, and construction materials may incur additional fees of $25–$100 per item. Always mention hazardous materials when you call for a quote.

4. Same-Day Service

Scheduling same-day junk removal in Oshkosh or Appleton typically costs 10–20% more than booking in advance. Next-day service is usually our standard rate.

5. Location

Most of our service area (Oshkosh, Appleton, Neenah, Fond du Lac, Menasha) is covered at consistent rates. Outlying areas like Green Lake or Waupaca may have slight surcharges of $25–$50.

Junk Removal vs. Dumpster Rental: What Costs Less?

Many people wonder if they should rent a dumpster instead. Here's the comparison:

Dumpster Rental: $300–$500 per week, plus delivery/pickup fees ($50–$100). If you keep it longer than a week, you pay daily overage charges ($5–$15 per day). You also have to load the dumpster yourself—hard, physical work.

Junk Removal: $150–$600 one-time fee, and our crew does all the heavy lifting. No loading required. No weekly fees. No overage charges.

For a typical garage or basement cleanout, junk removal saves you money and eliminates the physical strain. Dumpster rental only makes sense for large construction projects where you need to accumulate debris over time.

Ways to Reduce Your Junk Removal Cost

Want to save money on junk removal in Oshkosh or Appleton? Here are practical tips:

  • Consolidate Items: Move everything to one area so our crew can load faster. This saves time and reduces labor costs.
  • Schedule Off-Peak: Book on Tuesday through Thursday instead of weekends. You may qualify for a discount of 5–10%.
  • Schedule Next-Day or Later: Same-day service costs more. If you can wait until tomorrow or later in the week, you'll save 10–20%.
  • Remove Hazardous Materials Yourself: If you safely dispose of paint, batteries, or chemicals before we arrive, you'll avoid those special handling fees.
  • Donate Instead of Junking: If furniture or appliances are still usable, Clear the Clutter will donate them to our retail store (and you may get a tax deduction). This saves disposal fees.
