How do I prepare for a junk removal appointment?
Minimal preparation is needed on your part. The most helpful thing you can do is ensure our crew has clear access to the items being removed — move vehicles from the driveway if items are in the garage, clear a path through hallways if we're removing items from interior rooms, and if possible, gather smaller loose items together so nothing gets missed. If there are items in the same area that you want to KEEP, clearly mark or separate them so our crew knows what to leave. You do not need to move heavy items yourself — that's what our crew is for. You do not need to bag or box loose items — we can handle loose items as-is. If items are outdoors, ensure the gate is unlocked. If you will not be present, arrange for someone to provide access and designate which items to remove. The crew will text you when they arrive and when the job is complete.
