About Clear the Clutter Junk Removal

Locally owned and operated in Oshkosh, WI. Serving the Fox Valley with pride since day one.

Our Story

Built on Hard Work, Trust & Community

Clear the Clutter Junk Removal was founded with a simple mission: help people reclaim their space without the hassle, the heavy lifting, or the headache. Based right here on Bowen Street in Oshkosh, we're a locally owned and operated company that treats every customer like a neighbor — because many of them are.

What started as a small operation has grown into the Fox Valley's most trusted junk removal service, with a perfect 5.0-star Google rating and over 137 five-star reviews. But we haven't grown by cutting corners or raising prices. We've grown by showing up on time, working hard, being honest about pricing, and treating every home with respect.

Kevin and his crew are known for going above and beyond — from sweeping the garage floor after a cleanout to carefully helping families sort through the belongings of a loved one. That's the Clear the Clutter difference.
